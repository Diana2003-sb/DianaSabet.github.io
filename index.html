About us

Located on a beautiful private island with pristine beaches and lush tropical greenery, Maldivian Bliss offers a truly luxurious retreat in true Maldivian style.

A wide range of restaurants, large villas with private infinity pools, and our multiple award-winning Spa make Maldivian Bliss an excellent choice. A large variety of water sports, excursions, and further activities are available for our guests.
